Drop Shiping

The method of order fulfillment where a merchant does not store or ship the product they sell, and the entire product supply chain is managed by the manufacturer, is known as drop-shipping. The merchant forwards the order received from the customer to the manufacturer, who manages the delivery directly from their warehouse to the customer. This is a useful method of order fulfillment; drop-shipping allows retailers to manage multitudes of suppliers and guarantees a standardized customer experience.
