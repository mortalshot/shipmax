eCommerce Fulfillment Services

Our clients can easily connect their stores to our systems, which can regularly update the inventories once we receive products from suppliers for stores all over the U.S. We have a top-notch order management system regulated by inventory monitoring software that helps in receiving and managing orders. These orders are then fulfilled using our delivery and shipping network, which caters to products of every size and has access to the global market.

We can have your order picked up, packed, and shipped to your location without any delay. Our automated process also makes our services cheaper and provides a problem-free customer experience.

Drop Shipping

At ShipMax, we also provide drop-shipping services that give you flexibility and control over your business to expand and evaluate the market. With these drop-shipping services, our clients can concentrate on devising new strategies according to the market trends and developing new business channels and plans while we take care of all the supply chain and shipping concerns. We can even manage shipments from third parties for you through our order management software. The easy-to-use online platform lets our clients handle and overview the entire supply chain process through their dashboard and monitor every product and supplier.

Reverse Logistics: Return Management

With our Return Management service for Amazon and other marketplaces we make sure you don't incur losses on return products. This service can help you manage your returns correctly thus improving your ROI. Our system provides comprehensive return management services, including the inspection of all returned products, classification, restoring, and re-packaging. Our return management system provides photo-based products reports for you to analyze and make decisions. When products are returned by your customers we receive and process them, and then either send it back to your inventory or dispose of it, if required.

FBA Prep Service

Amazon FBA packaging requirements are tiresome, and some businesses struggle to fulfill them for their products. ShipMax is happy to manage this process, providing efficient packaging services to your business according to all required specifications. We ensure that the packaging of your products meets the strict Amazon fulfillment requirements. We help our clients keep their Amazon sales operations running effectively by simplifying the order fulfillment process, saving both time and money.

Our common practice is to unload products from an inbound carrier, relabel them, sort them based on the delivery destinations, and load them into their corresponding carriers. To ensure a simple shipping process and to benefit from major shipping discounts, we can also handle your consolidations and de-consolidations.

Mail Forwarding

We provide international shipping without paying an extra penny to top U.S. retailers. Our system uses virtual U.S. addresses for shipping purposes, and once the products are received through U.S.-based shipping agents, we deliver them to you anywhere in the world. Our system will update your account with progress details by uploading pictures of the transferred goods and posting tracking number details, which you can view online.

Our clients also have the option of sending several small shipments as one larger package, a service which is called consolidation, which saves considerably on postage fees.

Our courier partners – DHL, FedEx, UPS, USPS – comply with mail forwarding services and will deliver your product to any country on any continent through foreign outlets that we've outsourced for their shipping needs.
